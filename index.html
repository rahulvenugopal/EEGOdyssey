The Philosophy

This is not a passive course. Every day, you get your hands dirty — loading real EEG data, debugging real code, and building real intuition. We start with Python fundamentals and end with publication-grade statistics. No shortcuts, no magic boxes. Just you, the signal, and a terminal.
